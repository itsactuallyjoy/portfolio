My Journey into Tech

Published: August 2025

My journey into tech started with simple curiosity—wondering how the websites I visited every day were built. I had no background in programming, but I was eager to learn and experiment.

My first steps were small: learning HTML and CSS. At first, the syntax felt strange, but soon I realized I was literally creating something from scratch. That thrill of seeing my first “Hello, World” page pushed me forward.

Over time, I moved into JavaScript, which made my projects interactive. From there, I discovered Python and Django, which opened my eyes to the world of backend development and data analysis.

Looking back, I see that persistence, curiosity, and community support have been the biggest drivers in my journey. Every challenge became a stepping stone. Today, I continue to grow—building projects, exploring AI, and aiming to make technology that solves real-world problems.

Lesson learned: You don’t need to know everything to start. Just begin, stay consistent, and growth will follow.
